Game Instructions:
Battleships is a strategy based guessing game
You will play against the computer
The game is played on a 9 x 9 grid
You will each have 5 ships of different sizes
You will not be able to see each others ships on your boards
You will take in turns choosing coordinates
Ships will be placed horizontal or vertical, never diagonal
You will have unlimited ammunition!
When all enemy ships are sunk you win!

Your fleet:
2 destroyers : take 2 grid places each
1 sub : take 3 grid spaces each
1 carrier : takes 4 grid spaces
1 battleship : takes 5 grid spaces

Grid legend:
@ = Healthy ship
O = Missed target
X = Hit ship
. = Empty space

Good Luck!
